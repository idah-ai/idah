# Authors & Credits

## Ingedata

IDAH is an open-source project developed and maintained by Ingedata,
a company dedicated to providing high-quality AI training data solutions.

Ingedata's mission is to build expert-driven production teams that leverage cutting-edge
technology and advanced annotation tools to deliver precise, high-quality, and
scalable AI training data — ensuring cost efficiency and maximizing machine learning performance.

- 🌐 Website: [https://ingedata.ai](https://www.ingedata.ai)
